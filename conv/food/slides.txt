+ question: Some overweight people are not worried.
+ question: More people who are overweight are unhappy
than people who are not overweight.
+ question: Someone who is very heavy may not be
overweight, if they are big-boned.
+ question: Because there are people who are overweight,
being overweight is not a simple problem solved by exercise
and dieting.
+ question: People don't understand there is an easy solution.
+ question: Exercising is an easy solution to being overweight.

the mind off weight.

+ question: Exercise is not a good idea if you are underweight.


## Overeaters and undereaters

+ 1: Young American women are more likely to start
out undereating, than overeating.
+ 2: More men than women are affected by eating disorders.
+ 3: The causes are probably usually psychological,
rather than biological.
+ 4: A binge is a period of overeating.
+ 5: A binge is a happy period with friends and good eating.
+ 6: Treatment of eating disorders usually involves
eating medicine.
+ 7: Not many people recover from eating disorders.
+ 8: One form of treatment is talking to other people
with the same problem.
