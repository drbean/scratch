\newcommand{\myContent}[0]{

\begin{description}
\item [Questions about food]\

	\begin{itemize*}
	\item Have you had/Did you have breakfast?/Do you eat breakfast?
	\item What kind of food do you like to eat?/is your favorite?
	\item Can you tell/ask us about some food you know? Ask me/someone.
	\end{itemize*}

\item [Fruit guessing game]\

One person describes a fruit in English. The others try to guess what the fruit is.
eg passionfruit, bananas, mangos, apples. `It's green, or yellow. It's long and thin ..'

\item [The 4 snack food groups]\

Crunchy, chewy, creamy and bready.

\item [Questions about restaurants]\
	\begin{itemize*}
	\item Do you eat out a lot?
	\item What are the different kinds/names of restaurants?
	\item Tell us where/how/what you eat everyday at school/home.
	\end{itemize*}

\item [What to say at an expensive restaurant]\
	\begin{enumerate}
	\item 预约
	\item 进入餐厅
	\item 决定吃什么
	\item 订餐
	\item 甜点
	\item 要求检查/该法案要求
	\item 小费
	\end{enumerate}

\end{description}
}
