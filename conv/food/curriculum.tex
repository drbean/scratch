\large Questions about food
\begin{itemize}
\item Have you had/Did you have breakfast?/Do you eat breakfast? Ask me/someone.
\item What kind of food do you like to eat?/is your favorite? Chinese? Western? Steak? Cake? Fruit? Ask me/someone.
\item It's difficult to talk about food in English. Can you tell/ask us about some food you know? Ask me/someone.
\end{itemize}

\large Fruit guessing game

One person describes a fruit in English. The others try to guess what the fruit is.
eg passionfruit, bananas, mangos, apples. `It's green, or yellow. It's long and thin ..'

\large The 4 snack food groups
