Storycorps--American stories

Storycorps is an American project to record the stories of everyday Americans. You can hear them each Friday morning on NPR radio.
A lot of the stories are not difficult to understand. So they are suitable for people learning English. At the same time, they are real stories, moving, interesting, informative and likeable.

Tuning a piano at a nursing home

Ron Kroenke tunes pianos for a living. Here he tells the story of disappointing some people at an old people's home who had come to listen to some music.
