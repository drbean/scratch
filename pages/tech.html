Computer and Multimedia-Assisted Language Teaching

This course is about using the computer and multimedia to learn English.
Dr Bean thinks when we talk about the computer and multimedia we can talk indirectly about learning, something which is difficult to do directly.

Using the computer to sequence flash cards

Flash cards are/were small cards with a word on one side and the translation on the other. Nowadays the computer is used to space repetition of forgotten words more frequently and remembered words less frequently. http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Flashcard.

Sports/Fitness sublists: Theory, Activities, Weight training, Sports

We have learned 150 words in the 4 categories: Fitness theory, activities, weight training and sports for the midterm exam, using Anki or Mnemosyne. But first we need to establish what the base level is.

To do this, we will guess the meanings (in Chinese) of the words, or if we know them, just write them in.

Logging in

Note: You need to login to transcribe the conversation.

  • Your name is any name you want to use. English is good, Chinese is OK too. Your ID is your school ID.
  • The first letter of the ID, eg U or N, is a capital (big) letter.
  • Your password is the first half of your first (second?) Chinese name. It is in English, not the Chinese character. Thus JiaQi's pasword is Jia. See Lin YuTang's Chinese-English dictionary for the Hanyu Pinyin (漢語拼音) spelling.
  • If I was not able to find the first character in your name, your password may be a question mark. Eg, 賴?岑. Other people whose names my computer does not have Chinese characters for are 黃慧? and ?琮婷, but their passwords should be okay.
