Uniqlo Marketing Mix

  • Uniqlo

Business English students. Depending on whether you are A, B, C or D, I want you to listen to a different soundfile, again produced by the Festival text-to-speech system.

  • A students: Listen to a discussion of Uniqlo's product strategy.
  • B students: Listen to a a discussion of Uniqlo's thinking about price.
  • C students: Listen to how Uniqlo controls where its products can be bought.
  • D students: Listen to what Uniqlo does to promote its brand.

Can you understand how your part contributes to the marketing mix? How does your part relate to the other parts: product, price, place and promotion?

Then login and fill in the blanks in the uniqloparts exercise.
