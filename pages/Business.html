William Kendall

I thought the story of William Kendall and Green and Black Chocolates was very interesting. Forty years ago, macrobiotics or マクロビオティック, ie 長壽健康飲食法, was very radical. Now William Kendall has taken Craig Sams' idea of macrobiotic chocolates. and the two have become millionaires.

This week, I have made texts from information on the Internet about Kendall and his success with the New Covent Garden Soup Company and Green and Black Chocolates, and his ideas about involving everyone in a business's plan.

The sections are long. I don't want you to fill in every word. I have noted the sections which aren't so important and which you don't have to do. I will ask you to discuss the information in the important sections in Exam3 next week. You have until Friday night in Week 13, next week to finish the listening exercise.

  6. Green & Black's: Food Industry Factsheet
  7. Craig Sams - Welcome to my home page
  8. The Monday Interview: Craig Sams, 'one of Britain's greenest men ...
