Top Speaker's Advice

John Baldoni with 2 talks about public speaking.

Speaking students. Listen to John Baldoni speak about how to cure stage fright.
Do you know all the expressions he uses? the willies, butterflies, Go for it!, Break a leg!. Do you know, You have nothing to lose but XXX, where XXX is some bad or unimportant thing. Do you know what the podium is? I think this is good advice: Your challenge is to channel the nervousness you feel into energy you can put into your performance. Go for it!

Listen again to John Baldoni speak about what to say when you deliver a speech.
He is talking to business managers here, so what he says is right for delivering speeches at work, rather than elsewhere.
But his advice that you

  • 1) consider the situation,
  • 2) communicate what people need to know,
  • 3) use stories,
  • 4) translate things into benefits
    • ,
  • 5) synchronize your mind with your message and
  • 6) focus the audience's attention only on what they cannot get anywhere else,
is great advice for speakers in any situation.
