Speaking students. Listen to John Baldoni speak about how to cure stage fright.
Do you know all the expressions he uses? butterflies, Go for it!, Break a leg!. Do you know, You have nothing to lose but some bad thing. Do you know what the podium is? I think this is good advice: Your challenge is to channel the nervousness you feel into energy you can put into your performance. Go for it!
