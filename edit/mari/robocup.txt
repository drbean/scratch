Join the RoboCup Junior, World Tournament!



ABC Language School, in collaboration with the Association for Science Education, is providing educational programs to stimulate children's intellectual development in both the sciences and language and literature. Children love robots and robots help children develop their own potentials as they move and control them.. The ‘Robot and Language’ course helps children improve communication skills in English as a tool they need for future success at higher levels of schooling and in the workplace.



Children will also acquire:

Presentation Skills

In order to proudly present the functions and features of the robot they have built to children from around the world.



Challenging Spirit

In order to join the RoboCup Junior World Tournament and compete in its very dynamic soccer games, at times exchanging opinions and ideas with children of different nationalities.



Strong Leadership

In order to lead a team of children from non-English-speaking countries,
organized on the spot, to victory.



About RoboCup



RoboCup is a worldwide project that was launched in order to promote further
study in robotics and artificial intelligence. Its ultimate goal is to build
a robot soccer team that can beat the human world champion team in 2050.
RoboCup aims to help develop robot engineers who can contribute to international
society and it has attracted participants from 35 countries. RoboCup
strives to provide opportunities for collaborative learning that will help students and others gain the basic knowledge to become next-generation leaders, seeking 
‘cooperation’ before ‘competition’.




Course Descriptions

The ‘Robotics & Language’ course provides unique robot-English hybrid classes
to children who will be living in an evermore globalizing era.



The class is split in two parts.



Part 1: Robotics

Children, together with their classmates, work  on computer programming
which develops their logical thinking skills and intellectual creativity.



Children will learn:

・how to program an autonomous mobile robot

・how to find problems and solve them

・how to find their way to goals through trial and error



Part 2: English

Children work on delivering English speeches in order to develop the basic
communication and presentation skills in English that are essential for
success in the RoboCup Junior World Tournament.



Children will learn:

・how to deliver impressive presentations

・how to work and consult with people from other countries

・how to show leadership



Course Levels:

Basic Course (for beginners)

Intermediate Course

Advanced Course
