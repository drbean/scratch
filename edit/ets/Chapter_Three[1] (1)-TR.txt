Chapter Three
Methods
　　This chapter presents the research method for this study. This study aims to investigate vocational high school students’ anxiety, motivation, and attitude in English learning. This chapter has the following sections: research design, research hypotheses, participants, instruments, procedures, pilot study, data collection and data analysis.
　　
Research Design
  The study was designed to explore the vocational high school students’ anxiety, motivation and attitudes in English learning. The quantitative research method was used to collect and analyze the data in the study. The frame work 
was shown in Figure 3-1. 




Figure 3-1: The research structure of the study
Route of Research Concept 
Route 1 : Explores the differences in the learning anxiety with respect to students' background variables.
Route 2: Explores the differences in the learning Motivation with respect to students' background variables.
Route 3: Explores the differences in the learning Attitude 
with respect to students' background variables.
Route 4:  Probe Student's Perception relationship between Learning anxiety and Learning motivation.
Route 5:  Probe Student's Perception relationship between Learning Motivation and Learning Attitude.
Route 6:  Probe Student's Perception relationship between Learning Anxiety and Learning Attitude.

Research Hypotheses 
  According to the purpose of study and research questions, five hypotheses are presented as follows:
Hypothesis 1: There is no relationship in English learning anxiety of vocational high school students with different majors.
Hypothesis 2: There is no relationship in English learning motivation of vocational high school students with different majors.
Hypothesis 3: There is no relationship in English learning attitude of vocational high school students with different majors.  
Hypothesis 4: There is no relationship for the purpose of vocational high school students with different majors.  
Hypothesis 5: There is no relationship for the purpose of vocational high school students' careers ways with different majors.
Hypothesis 6: There is no relationship for the purpose of vocational high school students' advanced studies ways with different majors.
