Stress: CUSP!

A,B,C,D!

  • Question 1: A, B, C, and D all agree lack of control is causing them stress.
  • Question 2: A and D are feeling stress because of uncertainty.
  • Question 3: A, B, C, and D all agree lack of support is causing them stress.
  • Question 4: A, B, C, and D all agree they are under pressure and this is causing them stress.

Control, Uncertainty!

  • Question 5: Of the 4 people, only Ellen feels like she has control.
  • Question 6: Of the 4 people, all except teachers feel a lot of uncertainty about what is happening.
  • Question 7: All of the 4 people feel a lot of stress because of uncertainty.
  • Question 8: All of the 4 people feel stress because of lack of control.

Support, Pressure!

  • Question 9: Students have support, but Steve didn't have support.
  • Question 10: Teachers don't have pressure, but Ellen did have pressure.
  • Question 11: All of the 4 people feel a lot of stress because of pressure.
  • Question 12: One of the 4 people might feel stress because of lack of support.
